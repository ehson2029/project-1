Global Action

Reduced Inequalities

Sustainable Development Goal 10 (SDG 10) aims to reduce income inequality within and among countries by 2030. It focuses on empowering the bottom 40% of the population, promoting social, economic, and political inclusion for all, implementing policies for equality, improving financial market regulation, and facilitating safe migration.

Inequality affect everyone

Inequality limits opportunities and creates unfair systems. SDG 10 aims to ensure everyone has equal access to resources and opportunities.
