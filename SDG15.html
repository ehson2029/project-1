Protecting forests, wildlife, and biodiversity

Forests and Biodiversity

Forests cover about one-third of the Earth's land and are home to most of the planet's biodiversity. They play a vital role in absorbing carbon dioxide and regulating the climate.

Deforestation, illegal logging, and habitat destruction are causing rapid biodiversity loss worldwide.
