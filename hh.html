Global Action

Zero Hunger

End hunger, achieve food security and improved nutrition, and promote sustainable agriculture. SDG 2 is about making sure every person has enough safe and nutritious food to live a healthy life.

Hunger is a Human Crisis

Hunger is not just about not having enough food. It is about not having access to nutritious, safe, and affordable food. SDG 2 aims to end all forms of hunger and malnutrition by 2030, making sure all people have enough good food all year round.

"The world is not lacking in the resources to end hunger. What it lacks is the political will."

The Statistics

The numbers behind the mission.

733M
Go Hungry Daily

About 733 million people face hunger every day — roughly 1 in 11 people on the planet.

148M
Children Stunted

148 million children under age 5 suffer from stunting — they are too short for their age due to chronic malnutrition.

3.1B
Cannot Afford Healthy Food

Over 3 billion people cannot afford a healthy diet — making malnutrition one of the biggest health crises in the world.
