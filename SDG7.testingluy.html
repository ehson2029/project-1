The Renewable Revolution

The Renewable Revolution

Energy is the heart of every modern challenge. From jobs and security to climate change and food production, access to energy is essential. However, energy production remains the largest contributor to global warming.

Goal 7 focuses on expanding infrastructure and upgrading technology to provide clean energy sources in all developing countries. This means universal access to affordable electricity by 2030.

How can we help?

Switching to LED Light bulbs and choosing energy-efficient applicances are small steps that make a massive difference. Saving energy at home reduces the demand for fossil fuels.
