Protecting oceans, seas, and marine ecosystems.

The Importance of Oceans

Oceans are essential for life on Earth. They cover 70% of the planet and produce more than half of the oxygen we breathe. They also absorb large amounts of carbon dioxide, helping slow climate change. Protecting them is crucial for our survival.

"Oceans are the lungs of our planet. We must protect them."
