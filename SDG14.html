Protecting oceans, seas, and marine ecosystems

The Importance of Oceans

Oceans are essential for life on Earth. They cover 70% of the planet and produce more than half of the oxygen we breathe. They also absorb large amounts of carbon dioxide, helping slow climate change.

Millions of marine species depend on healthy oceans, and over 3 billion people rely on marine biodiversity for food and income. However, human activities are rapidly damaging these ecosystems.
