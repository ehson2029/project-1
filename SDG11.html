Sustainable Cities and Communitie

Make cities inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable.

Why Sustainable Cities Matter

Aims to "make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable" by 2030. It focuses on providing safe housing, sustainable transport, reducing disaster impacts, and improving environmental quality in urban areas, which currently house over half the global population.

Cities must provide safe housing, transportation, and green spaces for all people.
