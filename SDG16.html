Peace and Justice

Building peaceful and inclusive societies.

Why Justice Matters

Peaceful societies depend on strong institutions, fair laws, and equal access to justice. Corruption, inequality, and weak governance can undermine trust in society and slow down the development. Strengthening legal systems esures that human rights are protected and everyone is treated fairly under the law.

Many people still face violence, corruption and lack of legal protection worldwide."
