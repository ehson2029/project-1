Responsible Consumption

SDG 12, "Responsible Consumption and Production," is one of the 17 Sustainable Development Goals established by the United Nations in 2015. Its primary mission is to ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns, aimed at doing more and better with less, increasing resource efficiency, and reducing global waste.
