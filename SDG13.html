Climate Action

Climate action

SDG 13: Climate Action is one of the 17 Sustainable Development Goals established by the United Nations to "take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts". It focuses on strengthening resilience to climate-related hazards, integrating climate measures into national policies, and improving education and capacity for mitigation and adaptation.

Act now.

Climate change affects everyone. Immediate action is needed to protect our planet.
